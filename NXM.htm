NXM

Solargy E-Mobility Celebrates Successful Participation in the NXM (Next in Mobility) Event

We are delighted to share that Solargy E-Mobility successfully participated in the highly anticipated NXM event, held on October 1, 2022, at mall of Arabia, powered by Electrified.

The NXM event brought together pioneers and enthusiasts from the sustainable mobility industry for a day of engaging discussions, technological showcases, and exciting demonstrations. We were honoured to join this esteemed gathering and showcase our commitment to providing innovative solutions for sustainable urban mobility.

As an exhibitor at the event, our team presented our comprehensive range of Electric Vehicle (EV) charging stations, advanced charging points and parking integrated solution, and professional installation and maintenance services.

Our goal was to highlight our dedication to making the transition to sustainable mobility seamless, convenient, and accessible for businesses, governments, and EV owners alike.

During the event, we had the opportunity to engage with industry experts and share insights on the latest trends and advancements driving the EV revolution. We were thrilled to witness the enthusiasm and curiosity among the attendees, demonstrating a shared commitment to making our cities cleaner and greener for future generations.

Participating in the NXM event was a tremendous honour for Solargy E-Mobility, reaffirming our position as a key player in the sustainable mobility sector. We extend our deepest appreciation to the organizers, Electrified and the Greek Campus, for hosting such an exceptional event that created a platform for knowledge-sharing and collaboration.

We would also like to thank all the visitors who stopped by our booth and expressed interest in our products and services. Your support motivates us to continue delivering advanced, reliable, and sustainable EV solutions.
