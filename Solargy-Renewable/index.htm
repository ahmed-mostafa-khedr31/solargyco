On Grid Solution

The most cost-efficient solution for your home and business, CONNECT Kit is a system connected to the national utility grid. The on-grid solution generates power from a solar system, allowing you to store energy in the electric grid, when your solar system is producing more power than your consumption. The excess power can often be sold back to the utility in a practice known as net metering. when your solar system is not producing sufficient power or during night hours you use either the stored energy or consume from national grid, both ways, it helps in reducing the total utility bill.

Off Grid Solution

This independent solution is ideal for remote locations where access to the utility grid is not possible. This independent solution is preferred for villages, farms, buildings, and installations located at remote locations. A reliable power supply; even without a utility grid, the off-grid solution eliminates the problems of grid blackouts; providing energy 24 hours per day using different type of batteries as a backup (off grid) or Diesel Gensets as backup (Hybrid).
