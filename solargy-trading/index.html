About Us

Who We Are ?

Established in 2019, Solargy Trading specializes in the trade and distribution of solar energy equipment and E-Mobility solutions across Egypt and the Middle East. The company is in line with Solargy Group's vision for the development of the renewable energy and eco-friendly industry.

Our Vision

To transform solar-energy equipment and plants in Egypt and the region. We plan to revolutionize the trading of renewable energy solutions, electric vehicle chargers and smart parking as well as directly contribute to the development of the industry's perceptions.

Our Mission

To transform solar-energy equipment and plants in Egypt and the region. We plan to revolutionize the trading of renewable energy solutions, electric vehicle chargers and smart parking as well as directly contribute to the development of the industry's perceptions.

Our Products

At Solargy Trading, we offer a diverse range of high-quality products designed to meet the growing demands of the renewable energy sector. Our selection includes advanced solar panels, efficient inverters, reliable energy storage systems, and innovative electric vehicle chargers. We are committed to providing cutting-edge solutions that enhance energy efficiency and sustainability.
Solar Panels

ZNSHINE


ZNSHINE SOLAR is a globally recognized Tier 1 solar panel manufacturer and PV project developer, specializing in high-efficiency polycrystalline and mono PERC solar panels. With over 32 years of experience, ZNSHINE is committed to delivering top-quality solar solutions for residential, commercial, and utility-scale projects worldwide.

Solar Heater

Delpaso Solar


The Delpaso Solar Water Heater is an advanced and efficient solar thermal energy system designed for residential and commercial use. Manufactured in Spain, it is built with high-quality materials and cutting-edge technology to provide reliable and sustainable hot water solutions while reducing electricity consumption.

Inverters

Growatt


Growatt inverters are advanced and efficient solar power conversion systems designed for residential, commercial, and utility-scale solar installations. These inverters convert DC (direct current) electricity generated by solar panels into AC (alternating current) electricity, which can be used to power homes, businesses, and industrial facilities.

Cables

HIS


HIS Renewables is a leading manufacturer of high-quality solar cables and interconnection solutions for PV plants and power plants. With 20 years of experience, HIS cables are designed to provide maximum efficiency, durability, and safety in solar energy systems.

EV Chargers

Rolec wallpod


Manufactured in the UK, the WALLPOD is a low-cost charging unit, designed to offer full Mode 3 charging to every Electric Vehicle (EV/PHEV) on the market today. This charging unit has been specifically designed for domestic locations.

EV Chargers

Circontrol eNext


eNext has been designed to simplify the charging process. We developed an authorization method via app that allows the user to start charging without any interaction with the charger. In terms of the exterior design, we kept black and white as the core design colors while introducing curved lines and rounded shapes. The appropriate proportions and the perfect size, along with the piano black combined with matt white makes the eNext.

EV Chargers

Besen


A professional EVSE manufacturer, BESEN focuses on providing customers with professional charging equipment that is safer, more efficient and more stable. BESEN's products are oriented to the household and commercial markets in the EV charging field.

EV Chargers

Zerova


The Zerova AW32 is designed to exceed your expectations. Whether for residential or commercial use, our charger offers seamless wired or wireless connectivity, advanced user identification with an optional RFID card reader, and a sleek, customizable design that adds a touch of elegance to any space. With an IP55 rating for reliable use indoors or outdoors, you can trust our charger to withstand any environment.

Schneider Charge

EV Chargers

Sungrow


The AC22E-G2 charger by Sungrow is a reliable and versatile EV charging solution, featuring 30mA AC fault detection, EMC Class B protection, and IP55-rated durability for indoor and outdoor use. It supports 3-phase and 1-phase AC, OCPP 1.6J communication, adaptive load management, and control via an app, ensuring efficient and secure charging.
